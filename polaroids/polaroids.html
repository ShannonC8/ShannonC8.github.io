The secret to how polaroids works lies in its film. The image is reflected onto the film where chemicals in the film react to capture the image. This film is then pushed out by rollers whichbreak the seal on the chemicals which expose and develop the film making it visible within minutes. This type of camera is what I use to take my pictures. I find that the one shot nature of polaroid photos have a certain charm to it. Look through my pictures and see if you agree!
